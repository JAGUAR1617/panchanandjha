Overview

Researcher with 7+ years of experience in Robotics, data science, machine learning, AI, and IoT. Holding a Master of Technology and a PhD in robotics and machine learning, I am committed to staying updated with the latest advancements in AI/ML and Robotics. I possess sound knowledge in Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Swift, R-language, Tableau, Tableau Prep, Node- red, Heroku, Django, Streamlit, Flask, and MySQL. With my expertise and passion, I strive to solve real-time challenges and deliver outstanding results in every project.
